International Jobs

Conditions and Procedure for Application:

Conditions

  1. Must be 18 Years and Above
  2. Be conversant in English Language
  3. Must possess a valid passport
  4. Must have a clean police clearance
  5. Must have a COVID Certificate
NB:Credentials: Passport,CV,Academic certificates, colored passport photo of applicant

Application Procedure

  1. Complete the application form FULLY
  2. Pay job application registration fees (Ksh 5,000)
NB: Priority awarded to persons who have paid for registration

Process Flow

  1. Introductory engagement
  2. Present credentials
  3. Pay 50% of required commission
  4. Advised to attend full medical examination
  5. Undergo preparatory training session on expected standards in country of choice

Available Overseas Jobs

Receptionists,Store Keepers, Electricians, Guards, Cleaners,Hotel Val
